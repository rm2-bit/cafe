About Us

    • Our Story: A Decade of Dedication

    The journey of Cafe 365 began in 2010 with a singular, simple obsession: the pursuit of the perfect roast. What started as a humble neighborhood nook has matured into a cornerstone of the community—a place where the aroma of freshly ground beans meets the warmth of local connection, 365 days a year.

    For over a decade, we have traveled the world to source sustainable, single-origin beans from ethical farms. We believe that every pour should be an experience—bold, rich, and brewed with the precision of a craft.

    Whether you’re grabbing a precise espresso on your way to work or lingering over a creamy latte on a slow Sunday afternoon, our mission remains unchanged: to deliver an exceptional coffee experience with a personal touch.
